The Solution: Logitron

Logitron is an AI-based autonomous pallet lifting and transportation system designed to directly address the core challenges of industrial stockrooms. It automates high-risk, low-efficiency tasks, transforming the warehouse into a modern, data-driven environment.

Core Business-Facing Features

  • Autonomous Navigation: Optimizes routes to reduce bottlenecks and operational downtime.
  • AI Collision Avoidance: Minimizes accidents, reducing corporate liability (Law) and insurance costs (Finance).
  • Automated Pallet Handling: Ensures high accuracy, reducing costly stock errors and improving inventory management (Accountancy).
