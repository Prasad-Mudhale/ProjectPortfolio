About the Autonomous Wheel Weight Lifter

Concept and Purpose

The Autonomous Wheel Weight Lifter is an innovative engineering solution designed for industrial applications, automating the handling of heavy wheel weights in manufacturing and assembly lines. It reduces manual labor, enhances precision, and improves safety in high-volume production environments.

Autonomous Features

AI-powered navigation and decision-making for seamless, obstacle-free operations in dynamic industrial settings.

Sensors and Technology

Integrated LIDAR, ultrasonic, and vision sensors for real-time obstacle detection, alignment, and precise positioning.

Lifting Mechanism

Hydraulic lifting arms with adjustable grips, capable of handling up to 500kg, ensuring secure and efficient weight management.

Benefits

  • Increased efficiency and reduced operational downtime.
  • Enhanced worker safety by minimizing heavy lifting risks.
  • Cost savings through automation and lower insurance premiums.
  • Scalable for various industrial applications.

Real Industrial Use Case

Deployed in MIDC (Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation) industries for wheel assembly, where it has demonstrated reliable performance in real-world production lines, reducing manual errors and improving throughput by 40%.
